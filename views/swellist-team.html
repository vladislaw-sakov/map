"Explore Flushing"
Andrea P.

“A friend invited me and my boyfriend to an evening Mets game and I thought we'd check out that part of Queens beforehand. I had no idea what Flushing was about so was really surprised to get such a cool itinerary from Swellist. The day of it was great to pick from such thoughtful suggestions - not only did I get restaurant picks based on the foods I like, but they even recommended for some amazing gardens and temples (who knew!?!) and insider tips (like Chinese translations of menu specials) that totally impressed.
